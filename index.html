Rochers-de-Naye Forest Trail

9.4
777 Votes

Embark on the captivating Rochers-de-Naye trail from the heart of Montreux. Traverse through the dense Swiss forest and be enveloped by nature's beauty.

This enchanting trail climaxes at the summit of Rochers-de-Naye, offering breathtaking views of Lake Geneva and the Alpine panorama. An adventure not to be missed!

  • Start Point: Montreux Station
  • Duration: 5-6 Hours
  • Terrain: Diverse Landscapes
  • Difficulty: Moderately Challenging
  • Views: Lake Geneva, Alps
  • Versatility: Cogwheel Option
  • Summit: Marmot Park
  • Wildlife: Chamois, Foxes, etc.
